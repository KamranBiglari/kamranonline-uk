## Hi, I'm Kamran Biglari

I'm a **Chief Cloud Engineer** based in London, UK, with a passion for cloud computing, infrastructure automation, and building scalable systems. With over 15 years of experience in web hosting services and 12 years as a datacenter administrator, I've had the privilege of working on everything from designing private datacenters to architecting complex multi-cloud and hybrid cloud environments.
